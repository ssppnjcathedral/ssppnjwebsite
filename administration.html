The Church Hierarchy

Saints Peter & Paul Cathedral is under the omophorion of:
Metropolitan of All America and Canada
His Beatitude Tikhon
Archbishop of Washington, Metropolitan of All America and Canada

His Beatitude Tikhon is the Primate of the Orthodox Church in America, guiding the life of the Church across the United States, Canada, Mexico, and beyond.

Archbishop of New York & the Diocese of NY & NJ
His Eminence Michael
Archbishop of New York and the Diocese of New York & New Jersey

His Eminence Archbishop Michael oversees the Diocese of New York and New Jersey, of which Saints Peter & Paul is the Cathedral church. More information at nynjoca.org.

Acting Rector

Rev. Solomon Longo
Acting Rector, Saints Peter & Paul Orthodox Cathedral

Father Solomon serves as the Acting Rector of Saints Peter & Paul Orthodox Cathedral, providing pastoral care, presiding at the Divine Liturgy and all services, and guiding the spiritual life of the Cathedral community. He is available for pastoral conversations, confessions, and inquirer meetings.
