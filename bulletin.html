    Regular Schedule

    Saturdays: Vespers at 5pm, Confession afterward.

    Sundays: Divine Liturgy at 9:30am with Hours beginning at 9:10am.

    Bible Study is on Wednesdays at 8:00pm in-person and by Zoom.

    The Church is open for prayer and confession Wednesdays from 9am-5pm.
