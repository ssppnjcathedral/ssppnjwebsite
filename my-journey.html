    How the Library is fed

    Each session on the catechesis and bible study pages carries its own circle. Tap it to mark the session walked through. The circle fills with gold, the date is stamped beside it, and the row appears here. Nothing is lost between devices once you are signed in.

    How Your Notes are fed

    On readings, prologue, and bulletin pages, any verse you highlight or note you write saves against the date you read it. The hub shows the three most recent. The full archive is searchable by book, date, or source. Every citation links back to the original reading.

    How the Icon Corner is fed

    The Saint of the Day page and the Prologue carry a small bookmark. Saints you mark come here with their feast day, so the calendar can quietly remind you a few days before.

    Where the Notes Come From

    Saving a verse, from wherever you are reading.

    A small widget appears next to each verse on the readings, prologue, and bulletin pages. Save, highlight, or annotate without leaving the page you are on.
