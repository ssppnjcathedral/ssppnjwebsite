01

How the Library is fed

On catechesis and bible study pages, each session carries a small circle. Tap it to mark the session walked through. Nothing is lost between devices once you are signed in.

02

How your Notes are fed

On readings, prologue, and bulletin pages, any verse you highlight or note you write saves against the date. The full archive is searchable by book, date, or source.

03

How your Saints are fed

The Saint of the Day page and the Prologue carry a small bookmark. Saints you mark come here with their feast day so the calendar can quietly remind you before their commemoration.
