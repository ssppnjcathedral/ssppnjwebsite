January 4, 2026

Theophany Services — January 2026

We gather this January to celebrate the Baptism of Our Lord in the Jordan — Theophany, one of the Twelve Great Feasts of the Orthodox Church. On the eve, January 18th, we will hold the Great Blessing of Waters followed by a parish meal. On January 19th, the Feast Day itself, the Divine Liturgy will begin at 9:30 AM.
